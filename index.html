Overview


MC-SEARCH is the first comprehensive benchmark designed to evaluate Multimodal Agentic Retrieval-Augmented Generation (MM-RAG). Unlike traditional RAG, MC-SEARCH focuses on structured long reasoning chains where agents must navigate through vast knowledge bases to solve complex queries.
